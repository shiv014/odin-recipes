Curd Rice

What is Curd Rice

Curd Rice (Yogurt Rice) is typically a South Indian dish made of curd (yogurt), cooked rice, herbs and spices. The states where it is most popularly consumed are Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It is also quite common in Maharashtrian households.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt (curd)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of oil or ghee
  • 1/4 teaspoon of mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • A few curry leaves

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and let it cool down to room temperature.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the yogurt and salt.
  3. Add the cooked rice to the bowl and mix well.
  4. In a small pan, heat the oil or ghee over medium heat.
  5. Add the mustard seeds and cumin seeds to the pan and fry until the mustard seeds start to pop.
  6. Add the curry leaves, grated ginger (if using), and chopped green chili (if using) to the pan and fry for another minute.
  7. Add the tempering mixture to the bowl of rice and yogurt and mix well.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves (if using) and serve chilled.

That's it! Curd rice is a simple and delicious dish that's perfect for a quick and easy meal.
