Masala Chai

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2-3 teaspoons loose black tea or 2 tea bags
  • 2-3 whole green cardamom pods
  • 2-3 whole cloves
  • 1 inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Sugar or honey, to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, add the water and all the spices (cardamom pods, cloves, ginger, cinnamon stick). Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for about 5-7 minutes. This will help infuse the spices into the water.
  2. Add the loose black tea or tea bags to the saucepan and let it simmer for another 3-4 minutes. If you like your tea stronger, you can let it steep for longer.
  3. Add the milk to the saucepan and bring it to a boil. Be sure to keep an eye on it so it doesn't boil over.
  4. Once the milk has boiled, turn off the heat and strain the tea into your serving cup. Add sugar or honey to taste, if desired.
  5. Enjoy your hot and flavorful cup of masala chai!

Note: You can adjust the amount of spices and tea to your liking. Some people like more ginger or cardamom, while others prefer less. You can also add a pinch of black pepper or nutmeg for an extra kick of flavor.
